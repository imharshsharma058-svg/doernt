Surveillance & Access Control Systems (CCTV, Biometric, IoT)

CCTV Surveillance Systems

Our advanced CCTV solutions provide 24/7 monitoring and recording for enhanced security and situational awareness. We offer IP-based and HD analog surveillance systems with motion detection, night vision, and remote viewing capabilities. Designed for industries, offices, and public spaces, our CCTV systems ensure reliable security coverage and scalable integration with IoT and AI-based analytics.

Biometric Access Control

We deploy cutting-edge biometric systems that ensure secure, contactless access management for employees and visitors. Using fingerprint, facial recognition, or RFID technology, our access control solutions enable precise user authentication and entry tracking. Integrated with centralized databases, these systems provide real-time logs and analytics for workforce management and compliance reporting.

IoT-Enabled Security Integration

Our IoT-integrated systems connect all your surveillance and access control devices into a unified smart network. This allows centralized monitoring, remote alerts, and automated threat detection through AI-powered analytics. Whether managing multiple buildings or remote facilities, our IoT-enabled security infrastructure ensures full visibility and control over your premises.
