Who We Are

Dorent Services India Private Limited is one of India’s leading enablers of connectivity and communication solutions for businesses. Established in 2019 at Noida, Uttar Pradesh, Dorent has built a strong reputation for delivering reliable, sustainable, and technology-driven Electrical and IT Infrastructure projects nationwide.

Our services span across connectivity, collaboration, cloud, security, and enterprise solutions. We are committed to excellence, safety, and timely execution, having successfully delivered numerous projects that meet the highest industry standards for both government and private clients.

Our expertise lies in creating sustainable, energy-efficient, and globally connected infrastructures that empower organizations toward smarter operations and better productivity.
